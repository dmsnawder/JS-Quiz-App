Nightmares of Claire: Tower Defense Nightmares of Claire is a Tower Defense game I made in about a month. Claire's dreams have become nightmares after strange shadows have begun emerging from her closet each night to torment her as she sleeps. The player must take control of her guardians and keep the shadows from reaching her until 6 AM each night. There are 6 nights to survive on currently 4 different levels of varying difficulty.
Click the screenshot to play the game! (NOTE: Chrome no longer supports Unity Web Player. Any other browser should work fine.)

Technologies used: Unity Engine, C#, .NET, Photoshop.
